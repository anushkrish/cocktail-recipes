Pisco Sour
==========

Ingredients
-----------
* 3.5 oz Pisco
* 0.5 oz simple syrup
* 0.75 oz fresh lemon juice

Preparation
-----------
In cocktail shaker filled with ice, combine egg white, Pisco, simple syrup, and 
lemon juice. Cover, shake vigorously for 15 seconds, and strain into six-ounce 
cocktail glass.
